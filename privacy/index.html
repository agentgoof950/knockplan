Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how KnockPlan collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the KnockPlan mobile application and related services.

Information we may collect

Depending on the features you use, KnockPlan may collect or process:

  • Account information: such as your name, email address, user ID, and authentication information.
  • Prospect and route information: such as business names, addresses, route plans, visit history, outcomes, reminders, and follow-up details.
  • User content: such as written notes, voice notes, images, and other information you choose to enter or upload.
  • Location information: such as precise or approximate device location, when you give permission, to support routing and nearby-account features.
  • Device and technical information: such as device type, operating system, app version, diagnostics, and crash information.
  • Usage information: such as interactions with app features, when analytics or diagnostic services are enabled.

How we use information

We use information to provide and maintain KnockPlan, save and sync your work, support route-planning and check-in features, respond to support requests, improve reliability, protect the service, and comply with applicable legal obligations.

Location, microphone, and camera permissions

KnockPlan only accesses device permissions after you choose to allow them. Location may support routes and nearby-account features. Microphone access may support voice notes. Camera or photo-library access may support image uploads. You can change these permissions at any time in your device settings.

How information is shared

KnockPlan does not sell personal information. Information may be processed by service providers that support app hosting, authentication, databases, file storage, analytics, diagnostics, mapping, or other app functionality. These providers may only process information as needed to provide their services.

Base44 and other service providers

KnockPlan was built using Base44 and may rely on Base44 infrastructure and connected third-party services. Those providers maintain their own privacy and security practices.

Data retention

Information is retained for as long as needed to provide the service, maintain legitimate business records, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and meet legal obligations. Information may be deleted or anonymized when it is no longer needed.

Data security

Reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards are used to protect information. No method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Your choices and rights

You may be able to review or update account information, remove content, change device permissions, or request account deletion. Rights may vary depending on where you live.

Account deletion

You can begin an account-deletion request from inside KnockPlan, when that option is available, or by following the instructions on the Delete Account page.

Children’s privacy

KnockPlan is intended for business and professional use and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

International use

Information may be processed in countries other than the country where you live. Those countries may have different data-protection laws.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, visit the KnockPlan Support page and open a support request.

This policy should match the actual settings and integrations used by your live KnockPlan app. Update it whenever the app’s data practices change.
